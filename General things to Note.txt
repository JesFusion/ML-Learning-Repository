{
    how to change terminal location from view to editor:


    If you always want your new terminals to open in the editor area instead of the bottom panel, you can change a setting:

    Press Ctrl + , (comma) to open Settings.

    In the search bar, type: "terminal.integrated.defaultLocation".

    You'll see a setting called "Terminal > Integrated: Default Location".

    Change the dropdown from view to editor.

    Now, whenever you use the Ctrl + Shift + shortcut, new terminals will always open as a tab in your editor area.
